Aerosol Jet Printing

Figure: In this process, ultrasonically atomized nanoparticle ink droplets are carried from the ink reservoir to the printhead, where they are focused into an ordered stream for deposition onto the surface of a piezoelectric substrate. The SAW device functions by transforming an applied voltage into an acoustic wave that travels across the surface at a material-dependent speed of sound VSAW. Once it reaches a receiver, the acoustic wave is transformed back into a readable electrical signal.
